Is synthetic data worth generating?

A pre-registered study on Vietnamese scene-text OCR.

Code & data ledger The pre-registration The scorer

I built a synthetic data engine for Vietnamese scene text. At full real data it is worth nothing. At a 2,574-crop label budget it is worth ≈2,200 real annotations. And the machinery is not what paid.

The money figure

The synthetic-vs-real gap is monotone in the real-label budget and dies before full data.

TL;DR
  1. Full real data → synthetic buys 0.
  2. Scarce labels (10%) → synthetic ≈ 2,195 real crops.
  3. The degradation stack contributed ~6% of the gain.
